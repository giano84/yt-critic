YT Critic — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

YT Critic is a Chrome extension that analyzes YouTube video transcripts using Claude AI. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Anonymous usage token. A random UUID is generated on first install and stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. This token is used only to count how many free analyses you have used. It contains no personal information and cannot be used to identify you.

Video transcript. When you click "Analyze", the transcript of the active YouTube video is extracted from the page and sent to our backend server for analysis. The transcript is processed in real time and is not stored.

Video URL and title. The URL and title of the analyzed video are sent to our backend together with the transcript. They are used only to provide context to the AI model and are not stored.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information.
  • We do not track your browsing history.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.
  • We do not store transcripts, video URLs, or analysis results on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Anthropic Claude AI. Transcripts are processed by the Claude AI API (Anthropic, PBC) via our backend server. Anthropic's privacy policy applies: anthropic.com/privacy.

Railway. Our backend runs on Railway (Railway Corp). No user data is persisted on Railway beyond the anonymous usage counter.

Local Storage

The anonymous token is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted to any third party. You can clear it at any time by removing the extension.
